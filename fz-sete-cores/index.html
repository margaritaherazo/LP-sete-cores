About

Welcome to our slice of paradise!

In 2021, we purchased 10 hectares of vibrant jungle, located just 17 kilometers from the beach and the charming village of Serra Grande in Bahia, Brazil. This land is rich with cacao trees, a variety of fruit trees, and numerous native plant species. It also boasts abundant water sources, which we are committed to protecting.
Our vision is to rejuvenate this land through reforestation, as extensive tree cutting over the past 30 years has left its mark. We aim to cultivate thriving gardens and build a few cozy houses, creating spaces where people can reconnect with nature.
We invite volunteers and visitors to join us in this journey of restoration. Whether you're looking to immerse yourself in the tranquility of the jungle, contribute to our reforestation efforts, or simply enjoy the serene surroundings.
If you're inspired by our mission, you are also welcome to donate to any of our projects. Your support will help us protect and revitalize this beautiful piece of land, ensuring it flourishes for generations to come. Together, we can breathe new life into this land, creating a sanctuary where nature flourishes and people can experience the beauty and peace of Bahia.
